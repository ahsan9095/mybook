We Offer Building And Carpentry in London

Home Solustions London's fully qualified and experienced builders specialise in building works and general repairs. We can work from designs to professionally create the interiors and exteriors you want.

Our tradesmen can construct external and internal walls and carry out brick face repairs and pointing while our highly skilled plasterers can undertake rendering and plastering to a high finish. Floor screeding and leveling can also be carried out and we also offer full hard landscaping services such as block paving work, garden pathways, patios, garden walls and fencing. Major bathroom and kitchen refurbishments are also undertaken with larger works supervised by a quality control manager who will provide regular updates.
